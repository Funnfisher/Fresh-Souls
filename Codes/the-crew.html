Director: Wes Fisher Bio: Wes Fisher was born in Charleston, SC, and raised in Northern VA, just outside Washington DC. Fisher first studied film at the San Francisco Film Society, and then started his filmmaking career after joining Scary Cow, an independent short film co-op. He has been writing and directing short films since 2011. Fisher has won the Best Director Award from the Scary Cow Film Festival in 2013 and 2014.



Author: Kevin Wong Bio: Kevin Wong was born in Seoul, South Korea. He arrived the US and attended a film school at NYU in 2010. Wong has been in a movie industry since he graduated. He has wrote an enornemous amount of short films and two features. He loves writting about cultural documentaries and fictions.



Producer: Natasha Rich Bio: Natasha Rich was born in Sacramento, CA, and raised in the San Francisco Bay Area. Rich started producing film as an independent producer withshort film co-op, Scary Cow. She has been producing and writting feature films as well. In a free time, Rich loves working on movie posters/marketing materials.
